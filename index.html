About Myself

I began my journey in web development at the end of 2021 and joined an online remote bootcamp called Microverse in 2022. Throughout my time at the bootcamp, I gained a variety of skills, including essential soft skills. I collaborated with developers from around the world, working on projects through pair programming using the driver/navigator method. We reviewed each other's code, provided constructive feedback, attended morning meetings and afternoon stand-ups, and worked on individual tasks. In the morning meetings, I often took on a leadership role, leading discussions, giving instructions, and managing time effectively.

In 2023, I worked on several freelance projects. I listened carefully to my clients' needs, delivered on time, communicated clearly, and explained the development process in detail so they could understand each step.

At the beginning of 2024, I launched a YouTube channel to teach the basics of HTML and CSS to new developers. I also completed the 100DaysOfCode challenge, where I created front-end components and websites daily, sharing my progress on my channel. This challenge demonstrated my consistency and dedication to achieving my goals.
